Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu
High School
Tokyo Jujutsu High serves not only as a training ground for the next generation of sorcerers but a headquarters for all alumni who have graduated on to be full-fledged jujutsu sorcerers as well.
Meet class 1-A !
Yuuji Itadori
He was living a normal life until he encountered Megumi and ate one of Sukuna's fingers!
After becoming Sukuna's vessel, Yuji began attending Tokyo Jujutsu High alongside Megumi and Nobara as first-year students.
Megumi Fushiguro
The son of Toji Fushiguro and a descendant of the Zenin Family. Megumi was born outside the clan as Toji's trump card and was eventually found by Satoru Gojo, who mentored him and enrolled him at Jujutsu High as his student.
Nobara Kugisaki
She is a confident and brash young woman with an unshakable character !
She takes great pride in being both a pretty girl and a strong fighter, refusing to let anyone influence her.
Gojo Satoru
He is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world.
The Sensei of the gang and is the first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years and the pride of The Gojo family !
